# ROYAL DECREE OF THE PTOLEMIES
**Issued By:** Cleopatra VII Philopator, Queen of Kings
**Location:** Royal Palace, Alexandria
**Date:** February 1, 44 BC

## To: The Harbor Master of Alexandria

**Subject: IMMEDIATE EMBARGO ON GRAIN EXPORTS**

By the grace of Isis:

1. **The Crisis:** The Nile has flooded the Delta beyond the high-water mark. The harvest in Memphis is ruined.
2. **The Order:** No grain ships shall leave Alexandria for Rome until the granaries of Upper Egypt are full.
3. **The Cover Story:** Tell the Roman envoys that "adverse winds" and "structural repairs" prevent sailing.
4. **Penalty:** Any captain attempting to sail north will be executed.

**Signed:**
*Cleopatra VII*
