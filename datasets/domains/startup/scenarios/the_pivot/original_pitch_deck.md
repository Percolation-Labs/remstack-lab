# Pawsible - Series A Pitch Deck
**Date:** January 15, 2025
**Mission:** The LinkedIn for Pets.

## Problem
Pet owners feel isolated. They want to connect with other owners of the same breed but lack a dedicated professional platform.

## Solution: Pawsible
A mobile-first social network where every pet has a verified profile.
- **Features:** Sniff (Like), Bark (Comment), Park (Group Chat).
- **Monetization:** Targeted ads for premium dog food.

## Roadmap Q1 2025
- Launch iOS App Beta
- Acquire 10,000 DAU
- Partner with PetCo
